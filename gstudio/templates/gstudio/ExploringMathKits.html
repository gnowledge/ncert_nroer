Division of educational kits in NCERT has been developing science and mathematics kits for various stages of school education. Mathematics kits has been designed to enable the students to do various activities for learning mathematics concepts at upper primary and secondary level.The kit has a manual to enable the students to do the activities with kit items. Video programmes have been produced using these mathematics kits to increase the reach of these kits to the teaching learning community.
