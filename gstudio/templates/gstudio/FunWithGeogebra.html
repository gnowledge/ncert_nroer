Fun with Geogebra

Geogebra is a mathematical software which helps in teaching and learning of Mathematical concepts. The name comes from Geometry and Algebra.Geogebra is a very easy to use application and gives an opportunity to interact with geometry, algebra, tables, graphs, calculus and statistics. It facilitates the creation of mathematical constructions and models and allows interactive explorations by dragging objects changing parameters. We can create interactive worksheets using GeoGebra.

  • Area of circle
  • Angle in the same segment of a circle
  • Quadrilateral formation
  • Area of a triangle
  • Cyclic quadrilateral

Download Geogebra software on your computer or phone, create your own geogebra files
