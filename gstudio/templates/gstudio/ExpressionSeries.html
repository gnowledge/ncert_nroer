"Past is experience, Present is experiment and Future is expectation.
Use your experience in your experiments to achieve your expectations."

- Anonymous

 

The children of today are the future of tomorrow; this powerful statement brings with it, the obligation to mold and shape our children in order to create the best possible future for everyone. The experiences of past shall be used to experiment and achieve these expectations. Expression Series provides a platform to know more about the selfless and courageous services of the women and men who have served the nation in their own ways. The present day India has been possible only because, several women and men of yester years worked incessantly towards nation-building.

In this Expression Series one can watch audio and video biographies of eminent personalities and take inspiration from their lives to move forward and achive the unachieved.
